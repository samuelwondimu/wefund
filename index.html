Our story

Just like everyone else in the world, every Nigerian wants a roof over their head, they want to either rent an affordable home or own their own. But many are unfortunately, unable to do so ! There are real estate developers with land allocations across major cities in Nigeria but are unable to raise finance to develop the estates from the conventional bank due to high interest rate. There are Nigerians, living within and outside the country who would want to invest their money where it would be safe while yielding returns on a monthly basis. They often times, invest in the wrong hands.
